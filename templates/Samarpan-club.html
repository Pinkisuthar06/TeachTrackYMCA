About Us

SAMARPAN was established by a passionate group of electronics and electrical engineering students at YMCAUST with a shared interest in learning and showcasing technological innovation. True to its name, the club is committed to the spirit of dedication and contribution toward technical excellence.

All members are united by a devotion to learning and innovation, aiming to go beyond traditional ideas and techniques to build futuristic solutions.
