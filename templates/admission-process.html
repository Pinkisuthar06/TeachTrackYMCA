J.C. Bose University of Science & Technology, YMCA

Your Step-by-Step Guide to the Admission Process

1. Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet academic and entrance exam requirements for your desired program (UG/PG/PhD).

2. Entrance Exam

Appear in the required entrance exams like JEE (B.Tech), GATE (M.Tech), or university-level tests.

3. Online Registration

Register on the official admission portal and fill out the application form with accurate details.

4. Upload Documents

Upload scanned certificates, mark sheets, entrance scorecards, photos, and valid ID proof.

5. Pay Application Fee

Submit the application fee online using net banking, debit/credit card, or UPI.

6. Merit/Counselling

Participate in merit-based selection or counselling rounds (like HSTES for B.Tech).

7. Document Verification

Attend the verification process either online or on-campus to confirm eligibility.

8. Final Admission

Pay the first semester fee and receive confirmation of seat allotment and roll number.

Visit the official website for full details: jcboseust.ac.in
