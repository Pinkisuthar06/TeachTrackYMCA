About Us

IEEE YMCA SB is a student branch of renowned International technical and professional organisation IEEE. The YMCA Student Branch was founded in 2016 with the objective of providing students the exposure and knowledge about various technologies and opportunities to work, learn and grow.

Its main target is to try and test the new technologies and improve them. We have completed many projects so far and participated in various events and competitions at state and national levels. Some of them are hackathons, ideathons, and robotics competitions organized by IEEE branches of other institutions.
