Mechanical Engineering Branch Overview

B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering - YMCA Faridabad

About the Department

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at J.C. Bose University of Science and Technology, YMCA Faridabad, focuses on producing highly skilled mechanical engineers through a balanced curriculum of theory, hands-on labs, and project-based learning in thermal, design, manufacturing, and automation domains.

What You Will Learn

  • Engineering Mechanics, Strength of Materials
  • Thermodynamics and Heat Transfer
  • Fluid Mechanics and Hydraulic Machines
  • Theory of Machines and Machine Design
  • Manufacturing Technology and CNC
  • CAD/CAM, SolidWorks, AutoCAD, ANSYS
  • Refrigeration and Air Conditioning
  • Industrial Engineering and Robotics
  • Mechatronics, IoT in Mechanical Systems

Laboratories and Facilities

  • Thermal Engineering Lab
  • Fluid Mechanics Lab
  • Strength of Materials Lab
  • Machine Design and Dynamics Lab
  • Manufacturing Lab with CNC
  • CAD/CAM & Simulation Lab
  • Robotics and Automation Lab

Career Opportunities

  • Core Industries: Manufacturing, Automotive, Aerospace, HVAC
  • Design and R&D: Product design, SolidWorks, FEA/CFD (e.g., Mahindra, Tata, Ashok Leyland)
  • Public Sector: BHEL, NTPC, GAIL, DRDO, ISRO, Indian Railways
  • Higher Studies: M.Tech/MS in Thermal, Design, Robotics, etc.
  • Automation & Industry 4.0: Robotics, IoT, Mechatronics roles
  • Government Exams: IES, GATE, SSC JE, PSU recruitments
  • Entrepreneurship: Manufacturing startups, 3D printing, consultancy

Student Achievements & Events

  • Participation in SAE BAJA, Go-Kart, and drone competitions
  • Internships in Maruti Suzuki, BHEL, Hero MotoCorp, etc.
  • Workshops on 3D printing, ANSYS, AutoCAD, and Robotics
