Internet of Things (IoT)

B.Tech in IoT - YMCA Faridabad

About the Department

The IoT branch at YMCA Faridabad blends electronics, networking, embedded systems, and cloud computing to train students in building intelligent connected systems for smart cities, homes, industries, and health tech.

What You Will Learn

  • IoT Architecture and Protocols
  • Microcontrollers (Arduino, ESP32, Raspberry Pi)
  • Wireless Technologies (Wi-Fi, BLE, Zigbee, LoRa)
  • Cloud Computing and Firebase Integration
  • Real-Time Data Acquisition and Analytics
  • Cybersecurity in IoT Systems

Laboratories and Facilities

  • IoT Development Lab with sensors & kits
  • Networking & Protocol Simulation Lab
  • Cloud & Firebase Integration Lab
  • Smart Home Simulation Lab

Career Opportunities

  • IoT Engineer (hardware & firmware)
  • Embedded System Developer
  • Smart Infrastructure & Home Automation
  • Connected Vehicle Systems & Industry 4.0
  • Cloud Architect (IoT platforms: AWS, Azure)

Student Highlights

  • Projects on smart homes, wearables, health monitors
  • Internships at Bosch, Honeywell, Cisco IoT labs
  • Workshops on Firebase, NodeMCU, and MQTT
