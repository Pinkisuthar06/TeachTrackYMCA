About Us

Manan is the computer society of J.C Bose University. We aim to garner creativity and talent & propagate awareness regarding various tech-related knowledge throughout the ingenious minds.

We help students realize their potential by providing them a platform where they could show their skills and also a platform for learning and increasing their technical skills including coding, development, gaming, and much more.
