About Us

Microbird, the techno club, was established in 2010 to promote technical awareness in the field of electronics. We have been taking open sessions on various topics related to our learning so as to let people grow technically.

Our enormous participation in various zonal, national, and international events has helped us climb the ladder each year. We give students a chance to evolve technically while also providing them with hands-on experience.
