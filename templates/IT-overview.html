Information Technology

B.Tech in IT - YMCA Faridabad

About the Department

The Information Technology department at YMCA Faridabad focuses on delivering technical excellence in computing, networking, and data-driven technologies. With an updated curriculum and hands-on projects, the department empowers students to meet the demands of the IT industry with confidence and creativity.

Students benefit from highly qualified faculty, smart classrooms, industry projects, and tech clubs that support software innovation, coding culture, and data science research.

What You Will Learn

  • Computer Programming (C, Java, Python)
  • Database Systems, Operating Systems, and Networks
  • Web Development & Mobile Applications
  • Information Security & Cryptography
  • Cloud Computing & DevOps Practices
  • AI, Data Analytics & Machine Learning
  • Internet of Things (IoT) and Big Data Fundamentals
  • Project Management and Agile Methodologies

Laboratories and Resources

  • Data Structures & DBMS Lab
  • Software Engineering Lab
  • Cloud & Virtualization Lab
  • Cybersecurity & Network Simulation Lab
  • Web & App Development Studio
  • High-performance computing infrastructure

Industry Exposure

  • Workshops with AWS, RedHat, Oracle Academy
  • Seminars on DevOps, Cloud, Cybersecurity
  • Live Projects and Hackathons: SIH, Code Wars, HackNIT
  • Industrial training in Infosys, IBM, HCL, Capgemini
  • Internships through government and private R&D units

Career Opportunities

  • Software Engineer / Web & App Developer
  • Data Analyst / Business Intelligence Engineer
  • DevOps / Cloud Engineer (AWS, GCP, Azure)
  • Security Analyst / System Admin
  • UI/UX Designer / Product Tester
  • Startup Founder or Tech Entrepreneur
  • Higher Studies: MS, MBA, or M.Tech

Student Highlights

  • Active IT Club & Coding Society
  • Frequent coding competitions and hackathons
  • Campus placements in top IT companies: Wipro, Infosys, Cognizant, TCS
  • Research projects on cloud, IoT, cybersecurity

Eligibility and Admission

  • Admission through JEE Main (via HSTES counseling)
  • Minimum academic requirement: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • Merit-based seat allotment and reservation as per Haryana Government norms
