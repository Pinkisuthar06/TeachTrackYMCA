Electronics Engineering

B.Tech in Electronics Engineering - YMCA Faridabad

About the Department

The Electronics Engineering department at YMCA Faridabad provides in-depth knowledge of electronic circuits, embedded systems, semiconductors, and signal processing. The curriculum blends theory with VLSI, IoT, and communication technologies to meet industry demand.

What You Will Learn

  • Analog and Digital Circuit Design
  • Microprocessors & Embedded Systems
  • Signal Processing (DSP) & Communication Systems
  • VLSI, HDL & FPGA Programming
  • IoT, Sensors, and System Integration
  • Electronic Measurement & Instrumentation

Laboratories and Facilities

  • VLSI & Digital Design Lab
  • Embedded Systems Lab (Arduino, ARM, IoT Kits)
  • Analog & Digital Communication Lab
  • PCB Design & Fabrication Lab
  • DSP Lab with MATLAB/Simulink

Career Opportunities

  • Semiconductor & Electronics Companies: Intel, NXP, Qualcomm
  • Embedded Systems, Robotics & IoT
  • Defense R&D Labs: DRDO, ISRO, BEL
  • Communication & Signal Processing: Airtel, Ericsson, Nokia
  • Higher Education or Research in VLSI, Embedded, or AI/ML

Student Highlights

  • Projects on smart devices, IoT & wireless systems
  • Internships at STMicroelectronics, DRDO, and BEL
  • Tech fests and IEEE paper presentations
