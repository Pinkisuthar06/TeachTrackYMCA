YMCA Girls Hostel - Faridabad

Girls Hostel Facilities and Rules

Located near the main administrative offices of the University, Girls’ Hostel is an affordable, homely and safe accommodation with following facilities:

  • Mess Facility with hygienic food, inspected regularly, and police-verified staff
  • Well-ventilated and spacious rooms
  • Reading room, Common room with LED TV, Visitor room, and Guest room
  • Modern gym for physical & mental wellness
  • 24x7 RO water, ambulance and first-aid
  • Sports courts (Badminton & Basketball)
  • CCTV surveillance and security personnel
  • Biometric & manual attendance system
  • Sanitary Napkin Vending Machines and Incinerators
  • Hot water, cooktops, sandwich makers, newspapers, magazines
  • Wi-Fi in all wings and power backup
  • Fire safety, pest control and fumigation regularly conducted
  • Awareness lectures on hygiene and wellness
  • Programs: Self-defense, Anti-ragging, Career counseling, Yoga
  • Annual fresher party to foster bonding
  • Discipline rules with strict timings and ragging prohibition
  • Feedback, complaint registers, and suggestion box
  • Inspection and cleanliness reports maintained

Mess and Hostel Rules

The hostel mess is contract-based and managed by mess committees formed from the residents. Mess rules include:

  • Residents must carry hostel I-cards to the mess
  • Cooking in rooms is prohibited
  • Meals are served only in the dining hall during specified hours
  • Students must dress decently in the mess
  • Fine applicable for delay in monthly mess bill payment
  • Utensils must not be taken outside dining area
  • No outsider or proxy meal-taking allowed
  • Minimum of 25 days' mess bill payment is mandatory
  • Clearance required before leaving hostel after exams
  • Permission process defined for extracurricular events participation

Meal Timings:

  • Breakfast: 07:30 am – 09:30 am
  • Lunch: 12:30 pm – 02:15 pm
  • Dinner: 08:00 pm – 09:30 pm
