About Us

Founded in 2003, Srijan is the one and only fine-arts society at JC Bose University, YMCA. For over two decades, Srijan has nurtured artistic talent and creativity among students, maintaining its legacy and integrity.

Every year, we host 3-day auditions in September or October, including a PI round, to induct passionate and talented students. Guided by our beloved faculty mentor Dr. Preeti Sethi, we operate as a family, where every member is equally valued and celebrated.
