Electronics and Computing Engineering

B.Tech in Electronics and Computing Engineering - YMCA Faridabad

About the Program

The B.Tech in Electronics and Computing Engineering at J.C. Bose University of Science and Technology, YMCA, is an interdisciplinary program combining modern electronics with the power of computing. It prepares students for emerging fields like embedded AI, automation, data-driven electronics, and intelligent systems.

This course bridges the gap between hardware and software by integrating electronics design, signal processing, and programming with AI, machine learning, and cloud technologies.

What You Will Learn

  • Digital Electronics, Microprocessors & Embedded Systems
  • Programming in Python, Java, C++, and MATLAB
  • Data Structures, Algorithms, and Object-Oriented Design
  • IoT Systems Design and Smart Device Integration
  • Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning & Deep Learning
  • Signal Processing, Control Systems, and Robotics
  • Cloud Computing & Edge Computing Applications
  • Cybersecurity, Networking, and Linux-based Systems

Laboratories and Facilities

  • IoT & Embedded AI Lab
  • Advanced Computing Lab with GPU-enabled systems
  • Electronics & Communication Systems Lab
  • Robotics & Automation Lab with Arduino/Raspberry Pi
  • Signal Processing & Simulation Lab
  • Access to cloud platforms: AWS Educate, GCP, Azure

Industrial Exposure

  • Internships at Intel, STMicroelectronics, TCS, DRDO, and CDAC
  • Workshops on VLSI, Embedded AI, and Cloud-Integrated Systems
  • Live projects in home automation, smart healthcare, and drones
  • Industrial visits to semiconductor labs and smart factories

Career Opportunities

  • Embedded Systems Developer / IoT Engineer
  • AI-Enabled Systems Designer / Robotics Engineer
  • Software Developer / Firmware Engineer
  • Cloud Architect / DevOps Engineer (Hardware-Aware)
  • Cyber-Physical System Engineer / Research Scientist
  • Electronics Application Developer / Product Innovator

Student Highlights

  • Top positions in Smart India Hackathon (hardware edition)
  • Research papers in embedded AI and signal computing

Clubs and Innovation Groups

Admission and Eligibility

  • Based on JEE Main rank through HSTES counseling
  • Minimum 75% in PCM in 12th (as per AICTE/JEE norms)
  • Strong interest in hardware, software, and smart system design
  • Hands-on mindset and problem-solving attitude recommended
