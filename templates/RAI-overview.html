Robotics & Artificial Intelligence

B.Tech in RAI - YMCA Faridabad

About the Department

The RAI program at YMCA integrates mechanical systems, electronics, AI, and computer vision to prepare students for next-gen robotics, automation, and AI-powered solutions in industries, smart cities, and research.

What You Will Learn

  • Robotics Kinematics and Dynamics
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Computer Vision and Image Processing
  • Embedded Systems & Sensors
  • Python, TensorFlow, ROS (Robot Operating System)
  • Autonomous Navigation, SLAM

Laboratories and Facilities

  • Robotics & Automation Lab
  • AI & Deep Learning Lab (GPU-enabled)
  • Embedded & Sensor Lab
  • Computer Vision Simulation Lab

Career Opportunities

  • AI/ML Engineer in Robotics or Automation firms
  • Autonomous Vehicle Companies (e.g., Tesla, Tata)
  • Robotics Engineer (ABB, Boston Dynamics, etc.)
  • Research in AI, NLP, Human-Robot Interaction
  • Entrepreneurship in drone/robotic startups

Student Highlights

  • Drone-making, AI-powered bots, and ML projects
  • Internships in TCS, DRDO, AI startups
  • Participation in Smart India Hackathon & e-Yantra
