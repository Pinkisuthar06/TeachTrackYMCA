Computer Engineering

B.Tech in Computer Engineering - YMCA Faridabad

About the Department

The Department of Computer Engineering at J.C. Bose University of Science and Technology, YMCA, is dedicated to developing skilled professionals in the field of computing. The department offers a comprehensive B.Tech program covering core areas of software and hardware, emerging technologies, and industry-relevant tools and practices.

It has a strong emphasis on project-based learning, internships, and research opportunities. Students are trained in real-world applications of programming, software development, AI, and cloud computing through advanced labs and collaborations with industries and research institutions.

What You Will Learn

  • Foundational Programming (C, C++, Java, Python)
  • Data Structures, Algorithms, and Problem Solving
  • Operating Systems, DBMS, Computer Networks
  • Web Technologies, Mobile App Development
  • Cloud Computing (AWS, GCP), DevOps Fundamentals
  • Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science
  • Software Engineering, Testing & Agile Methodologies
  • Cybersecurity, Blockchain, and IoT basics

Laboratories and Resources

  • Programming & Data Structure Labs
  • Cloud and DevOps Simulation Environment
  • Machine Learning & AI Lab (with GPU-enabled systems)
  • Web & Mobile App Development Lab
  • Open Source Software and Cybersecurity Lab
  • High-Speed Internet, Projectors, Smart Boards, Git Integration

Industrial Exposure

  • Industrial Visits to IT Parks, DRDO, NIC, and STPI Centers
  • Internships at TCS, Infosys, Wipro, DRDO, Microsoft, etc.
  • Workshops in collaboration with NASSCOM, IBM, AWS Academy
  • Guest lectures by IIT professors and tech entrepreneurs

Career Opportunities

  • Software Developer / Full Stack Web Developer
  • Data Analyst / Machine Learning Engineer
  • Cybersecurity Analyst / Ethical Hacker
  • DevOps Engineer / Cloud Architect
  • Mobile App Developer (Android/iOS)
  • System Administrator / Network Engineer
  • Product Manager / Technical Consultant
  • Higher Studies: M.Tech, MS, MBA, or Ph.D.

Student Achievements

  • Winners in national hackathons: SIH, HackHarayana, CodeFun
  • GSoC (Google Summer of Code) selections
  • Placements in Google, Microsoft, Deloitte, Amazon, Capgemini
  • Student-led startups in edtech, e-commerce, and SaaS domains

Clubs and Activities

  • CodeChef YMCA Chapter and Google Developer Student Club (GDSC)
  • ACM, IEEE Student Branch
  • Open Source, Robotics & AI Clubs
  • Annual tech fest: “CONVERGENCE” & inter-university coding events

Admission and Eligibility

  • Through JEE Main counseling (HSTES)
  • Minimum 75% in PCM (as per AICTE/JEE norms)
  • No subject backlog at the time of admission
  • Strong preference to students with programming/coding background
