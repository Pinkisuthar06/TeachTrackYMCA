B.Tech Admission Eligibility Criteria

Through HSTES Counseling | YMCA Faridabad
Step-by-Step Eligibility Instructions
1. Academic Qualification

Passed 10+2 with Physics & Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with one of Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology / Technical Vocational subjects.

2. Minimum Marks

Minimum 45% marks (General) or 40% (Reserved) in the above subjects combined.

3. Entrance Test

Must have a valid JEE Main score conducted by NTA for the current session.

4. Counseling Registration

Register on the HSTES portal and pay the counseling fee to be eligible for allotment.

5. Seat Quota

85% seats reserved for Haryana Domicile; 15% open to All India candidates.

6. Special Categories

Seats reserved for TFW (Tuition Fee Waiver), KM (Kashmiri Migrants), EWS, etc. with relevant certificates.

7. Document Requirements

Upload scanned copies of 10th & 12th marksheets, JEE scorecard, domicile certificate (if applicable), and photo ID.

8. Official Website

All updates, schedule, and choice filling will be done via hstes.org.in.

Apply via HSTES Portal
