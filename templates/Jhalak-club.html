About Us

We are the photography and designing society of JC Bose UST, established in 2012. Jhalak provides the platform to bring together like-minded people and share knowledge about the art and concept of photography and designing.

You can't expect your camera to take great photos if your imagination is out of focus. Jhalak is not just for serious photographers or people with big expensive cameras!

The goal of Jhalak at JC Bose UST is to provide a place where people can come together to learn, share, and have fun with photography, filmmaking, and designing!
