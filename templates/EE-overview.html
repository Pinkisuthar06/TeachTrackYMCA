Electrical Engineering

B.Tech in Electrical Engineering - YMCA Faridabad

About the Department

The Department of Electrical Engineering at J.C. Bose University of Science and Technology, YMCA Faridabad offers a curriculum focusing on power systems, electrical machines, high voltage, control, and renewable energy systems with advanced simulations and industrial exposure.

What You Will Learn

  • Electrical Machines
  • Power Systems & Protection
  • Control Systems
  • Switchgear and High Voltage Engineering
  • Renewable Energy & Power Electronics
  • MATLAB, PSCAD, and SCADA Systems
  • Electrical Measurements & Instrumentation

Laboratories and Facilities

  • Electrical Machines Lab
  • Power System Simulation Lab
  • High Voltage Lab
  • Electrical Measurement Lab
  • Control and Relay Labs

Career Opportunities

  • Power sector companies: NTPC, PowerGrid, NHPC
  • Electrical utilities & smart grid projects
  • Core PSU jobs through GATE & IES
  • Industrial automation and energy auditing
  • Research in renewable and power systems

Student Highlights

  • Internships at power plants and sub-stations
  • Workshops on SCADA, PSCAD, and AutoCAD Electrical
  • Industrial visits to HVPNL, NTPC, BHEL
