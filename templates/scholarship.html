SC and BC Post Matric Scholarship

Applicable for B.Tech, M.Tech, BA, MA, BCA, MBA, etc. for SC and BC students with annual family income ≤ ₹2.5 Lacs. Reimbursement of tuition fee & allowances with 75% attendance. Forms available in Nov/Dec.

Click here

Merit-Cum-Means Scholarship

15% of B.Tech/MCA/MBA students eligible @ ₹750/month. Based on parent's income and academic performance. Applicable for Haryana domicile or CCB admitted students.

Fee Assistance to Economically Weaker Students

Soft loan by The MOB (Alumni Association) for cases like parent death, illness, job loss, etc. Application opens every semester.

Vishwa Prakash Mission Scholarship

Merit-Cum-Means based support since 2016-17. Notification issued during October-November each year.

University Medals

  • Governor/Chancellor Gold Medal: ₹75,000 + Medal + Certificate (Topper - B.Tech)
  • Chief Minister Gold Medal: ₹65,000 + Medal + Certificate (Topper Girl - B.Tech)
  • Vice Chancellor Gold Medal: UG & PG toppers in each faculty (Academics + Co-curricular)
  • Dean Certificate of Merit: Branch/Course wise topper (Min. 2-year program)
* One student can get only one award. Criteria pre-decided by university committee.
