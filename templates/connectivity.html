How to Reach JC Bose University of Science & Technology, YMCA Faridabad

Connectivity from Airport, Railway Station, Metro, and Bus Stand

Indira Gandhi Intl. Airport

Distance: ~42 km

Route: Airport Metro → New Delhi (Yellow Line) → Kashmere Gate → Violet Line → YMCA Chowk.

Best Option: Metro (Affordable & Safe)

New Delhi Railway Station

Distance: ~35 km

Yellow Line from NDLS → Kashmere Gate → Violet Line → YMCA Chowk.

Best Option: Metro (Avoid Traffic)

Escort Mujesar Metro

Distance: ~1.2 km

One station before YMCA Chowk. Auto/e-rickshaws easily available.

Best Option: Short auto ride (~₹10–20)

Faridabad Bus Stand

Distance: ~4.2 km

Take shared auto or cab. ~15–20 min depending on traffic.

Best Option: Auto/Cab
