Alumni Engagement & Excellence

Since its founding in 1969 as part of an Indo‑German initiative, YMCA Faridabad has grown into an A+ NAAC‑accredited university with a legacy of practical, industry‑oriented education.

Expert alumni such as Sanjay Mittal, Nitin Rastogi, A.K. Sen, and Rajesh Gupta have delivered key sessions in 2019 on modern power systems, HVAC, project management, and more—bridging academic learning and industry practices.

The campus fosters a vibrant extracurricular culture through clubs and societies like Ananya (literary & debate), Vividha (drama), Manan (technical), and various sports teams. These platforms have consistently nurtured future leaders and innovators.
