Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries about YMCA Faridabad

The full name is J.C. Bose University of Science and Technology, YMCA, Faridabad.

Yes, it is a Haryana State Government University offering various undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

YMCA offers B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, MCA, BBA, BCA, B.Sc, and Ph.D. programs in various disciplines.

Yes, YMCA Faridabad is typically considered a Tier 2 engineering institute with good placements and a strong academic record.

Yes, the university facilities of hostel for only girls with essential amenities and mess facilities.

YMCA is located at Sector 6, Mathura Road, Faridabad, Haryana – 121006.

YMCA has a strong placement record with companies like Infosys, TCS, Wipro, IBM, and many startups hiring from campus.

Yes, YMCA Faridabad is approved by AICTE and accredited by NAAC with an ‘A’ grade. It also has NBA accreditation for various B.Tech programs.

Admission to B.Tech is based on JEE Main scores followed by Haryana State Technical Education Society (HSTES) counselling.

Yes, several merit-based and need-based scholarships are available, including state scholarships, AICTE schemes, and scholarships for reserved categories.

Yes, industrial training and internships are an integral part of the curriculum. The university helps students connect with industries through its training cell.

Yes. YMCA organizes multiple events like Culmyca (Cultural Fest), Elements (Tech Fest), and various departmental events throughout the year.

The average package for B.Tech students ranges from ₹7-8 LPA, with some top companies offering up to ₹60 LPA depending on the branch and profile.

Yes, YMCA has an active E-Cell that promotes innovation and startup culture through workshops, competitions, and mentorship programs.

The campus includes modern classrooms, computer labs, hostels, library, auditorium, sports complex, cafeteria, medical facilities, and Wi-Fi access.

There is no strict uniform for most courses, but students are expected to wear formal attire during labs, presentations, and placements.

Yes. The university has a secure campus with 24x7 security, CCTV surveillance, dedicated girls' hostels, and a Women’s Grievance Redressal Cell.
