Civil Engineering Branch Overview

B.Tech in Civil Engineering - YMCA Faridabad

About the Department

The Department of Civil Engineering at J.C. Bose University of Science and Technology, YMCA Faridabad, is committed to producing industry-ready engineers equipped with strong theoretical and practical knowledge in the fields of infrastructure, environmental engineering, construction, and geotechnical engineering.

What You Will Learn

  • Structural Analysis and Design
  • Building Materials and Construction Techniques
  • Transportation and Highway Engineering
  • Hydraulics and Water Resources Engineering
  • Geotechnical Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Surveying and GIS
  • Construction Project Management
  • Concrete Technology and Steel Structures

Laboratories and Facilities

  • Structural Engineering Lab
  • Geotechnical Engineering Lab
  • Fluid Mechanics & Hydraulics Lab
  • Transportation Engineering Lab
  • Environmental Engineering Lab
  • Surveying Lab with Total Station & GPS
  • Material Testing Lab

Career Opportunities

  • Construction & Infrastructure: L&T, Shapoorji Pallonji, DLF, etc.
  • Government Jobs: PWD, NHAI, CPWD, Railways, Irrigation Department
  • PSUs & Exams: IES, SSC JE, GATE, BARC, NTPC, IOCL, etc.
  • Design & Consultancy: Civil design firms, project management consultancies
  • Research & Higher Education: M.Tech, MS, Ph.D. in Civil or interdisciplinary areas
  • Real Estate & Urban Planning: Smart Cities, sustainability projects
  • Entrepreneurship: Construction firms, green architecture, GIS startups

Student Achievements & Events

  • Participation in Smart India Hackathon (SIH), Baja SAE, and Concrete Cube Competitions.
  • Site visits to metro construction, water treatment plants, and bridge construction sites.
  • Internships with DMRC, CPWD, L&T, and leading civil firms.
